# Visual references

This registry is the project-owned place for screenshots, product references, photos, mood images, golden states, Mobbin-style pattern references, and design artifacts that should influence future UI work.

Do not bundle stock photos or large image sets with the design contract. Add only project-approved references that are useful for future design decisions. Store local files under `design/references/` or link to a permissioned source, then describe why each reference matters.

## Reference policy

- **Bundled with package:** no
- **Required for adoption:** no
- **Recommended starter set:** 3-10 high-signal references after the product direction is known
- **Maximum useful task set:** prefer the smallest set that covers the surface being changed
- **AI usage:** inspect applicable references before designing or visually modifying a surface; cite which references were used in the design brief
