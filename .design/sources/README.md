# Source provenance

These files preserve the evidence used to derive the operational contract. Ordinary design and implementation follows the compiled target contract and verified project mappings, not the raw source archive.

## Authority

1. `PRIMARY.md` and its ordered parts preserve the current Warm Paper Workbench visual/application source.
2. `DESIGN-PRINCIPLES.md` preserves the Calm Capability judgment, trust, review, and agent-design source that was canonical before consolidation.
3. `APPLE-DESIGN.md` and its ordered parts preserve compatible global interaction principles and principal Apple-specific guidance.
4. `ANIMATION-AUDIT.md` preserves the specialized read-only animation-audit workflow.
5. `RESEARCH.md` records supporting official specifications and their adopted implications.
6. `COVERAGE.md` maps source concerns to the operational owners that implement them.

The primary and Apple source archives are split into ordered plaintext parts to remain inspectable and installable. The manifest records part hashes and aggregate hashes so validation can reconstruct and verify the original byte streams.

A raw source may explain a decision but does not silently override the authority order in `../INDEX.md`. Durable engine changes must be operationalized in the owning global, component, pattern, vertical, quality, or governance contract. Project-specific specialization belongs in the consuming repository’s root `DESIGN.md` and `design/` façade.
