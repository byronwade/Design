# Web marketing overlay

Marketing surfaces explain, prove, and convert. They inherit product identity but not the density or shell of an operational application.

## Narrative structure

Lead with a specific audience, problem, value, and principal conversion. Follow with evidence, capability, workflow, trust, and clear next steps. Avoid generic hero language and feature grids with no prioritization.

## Layout and typography

Use stronger editorial scale, larger spacing transitions, controlled reading measures, and media that demonstrates real product value. Maintain fast scanning and mobile reflow. Do not turn every paragraph into a card.

## Navigation and conversion

Global marketing navigation remains shallow and stable. One primary conversion dominates a region. Secondary paths such as documentation, pricing, or sign-in remain clear without competing visually.

## Performance and discovery

Use semantic content, meaningful headings, metadata, crawlable links, responsive images, stable layout, and restrained scripts. Core information and conversion do not depend on animation or client-only rendering.

## Motion

Motion may support storytelling and rare delight but remains optional, performant, and reduced-motion aware. It does not delay reading, obscure claims, or imitate an application interaction that is not actually available.

## Prohibitions

Do not apply dense app sidebars, operational row heights, command palettes, or inspector patterns to a content page without a real recurring task.
