## Status vocabulary

Define the actual underlying condition and evidence for every product-specific status. Never use “done,” “live,” “synced,” “verified,” or “deployed” unless the state model supports that claim.

## Voice constraints

- direct, specific, calm, and respectful
- sentence case unless the target platform convention requires otherwise
- clear verbs for actions and concrete destinations for navigation
- name the object, scope, consequence, and recovery for high-impact actions
- do not anthropomorphize automation in ways that obscure capability, uncertainty, or responsibility
