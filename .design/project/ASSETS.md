## Rules

- use one coherent icon family per product surface and platform-standard symbols for established native meaning
- preserve aspect ratio, optical alignment, stroke/fill conventions, and crisp rendering at supported scales
- define light/dark/high-contrast and active/inactive variants where required
- record licensing, attribution, privacy, localization, and content-moderation constraints
- provide useful alternative text or mark decorative assets correctly
- reserve space and fallbacks so missing or delayed media does not destroy layout
- do not generate or substitute brand, legal, trust, product, or instructional assets without explicit approval
